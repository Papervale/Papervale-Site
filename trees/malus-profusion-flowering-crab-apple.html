About This Tree

The Malus 'Profusion', commonly known as the Flowering Crab Apple, is a stunning ornamental tree that brings vibrant colour and beauty to any landscape. This deciduous tree is admired for its profuse, deep pink to red blossoms that appear in spring, creating a spectacular floral display. The flowers are followed by small, dark red to purple crab-apples that persist into winter, providing food for birds and adding seasonal interest.
Key features of the Malus 'Profusion' include its broad, spreading canopy and attractive, purple-tinged foliage that turns to a rich bronze-green as the season progresses. At maturity, this tree typically reaches a height of approximately 5 to 6 meters and a similar spread, making it an excellent choice for medium-sized gardens or as a focal point in larger landscapes.

This Flowering Crab Apple thrives in well-drained, loamy soil but is adaptable to a range of soil types, including clay and sandy soils. It prefers full sun to partial shade, ensuring optimal flowering and fruiting. Additionally, Malus 'Profusion' is relatively low-maintenance and resistant to many common apple diseases, making it a resilient and rewarding addition to any garden.
