About This Tree

Carpinus betulus 'Rockhampton Red', commonly known as Hornbeam, is a striking deciduous tree noted for its attractive foliage and robust growth. This variety is particularly admired for its vibrant red new leaves that gradually mature to a rich green in the summer before transforming into a stunning array of yellows and oranges in the autumn. One of its key features is the smooth, grey bark that adds visual interest even in the winter months when the tree is bare.

At maturity, Carpinus betulus 'Rockhampton Red' typically reaches a height of 10 to 15 meters and has a spread of about 6 to 8 meters, making it a medium-sized tree suitable for both urban and rural settings. Its dense, oval crown provides excellent shade and makes it a popular choice for hedging and screening purposes. The tree is also known for its hardiness and ability to withstand pruning, which allows for various landscape uses.

This Hornbeam variety thrives in a range of soil types, including clay, loam, and sandy soils, provided they are well-drained. It prefers full sun to partial shade, making it quite versatile in different garden settings. Additionally, Carpinus betulus 'Rockhampton Red' is relatively low-maintenance and resistant to many common pests and diseases, making it an excellent choice for both novice and experienced gardeners. Its adaptability, combined with its stunning seasonal foliage, makes it a standout addition to any landscape.
