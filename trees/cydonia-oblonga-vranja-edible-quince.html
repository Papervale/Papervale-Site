About This Tree

Cydonia oblonga 'Vranja', commonly known as the Edible Quince, is a deciduous tree or large shrub that is celebrated for its fragrant, pear-shaped fruits and attractive, gnarled branches. This hardy plant typically reaches a mature height of 4-6 meters, with a spread of approximately 4-5 meters, making it an excellent choice for smaller gardens or as a feature plant in larger landscapes.

One of the key features of the 'Vranja' quince is its striking, golden-yellow fruits that ripen in late autumn. These fruits are highly aromatic and can be used in a variety of culinary applications, such as jellies, preserves, and even savoury dishes. The tree itself boasts beautiful, pale pink to white blossoms in the spring, which not only enhance its aesthetic appeal but also attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The 'Vranja' quince thrives in well-drained soil with a preference for slightly acidic to neutral pH levels. It is adaptable to a range of soil types, including loam, clay, and sandy soils, as long as they are well-draining. For optimal growth, this quince variety prefers full sun but can tolerate partial shade. Regular watering is essential, especially during dry periods, to support healthy fruit production. With its combination of ornamental beauty and practical fruit-bearing qualities, Cydonia oblonga 'Vranja' is a delightful addition to any garden.
