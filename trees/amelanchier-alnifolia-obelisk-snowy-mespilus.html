About This Tree

The Amelanchier alnifolia 'Obelisk,' commonly known as Snowy Mespilus, is a striking deciduous tree that brings a touch of elegance to any landscape. This variety is particularly admired for its narrow, columnar growth habit, making it an excellent choice for small gardens or as a vertical accent in larger spaces. At maturity, the 'Obelisk' typically reaches a height of around 4 to 6 meters, with a spread of approximately 1.5 to 2 meters, which makes it a manageable and versatile option for many garden settings.

Key features of the Snowy Mespilus include its stunning display of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom profusely in spring, creating a snow-like effect against the fresh green foliage. These blossoms are not only visually appealing but also attract pollinators, adding life and movement to your garden. Following the floral display, the tree produces small, dark purple berries that are edible and can be a delightful treat for both humans and wildlife. In autumn, the leaves transform into a vibrant mix of orange and red, providing a spectacular seasonal show.

The Amelanchier alnifolia 'Obelisk' is quite adaptable regarding soil types, thriving best in well-drained, fertile soils. It prefers a location that receives full sun to partial shade, ensuring the best growth and flower production. This variety is also known for its resilience, displaying good resistance to common pests and diseases. Additionally, it is relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal pruning to maintain its shape. Overall, the 'Obelisk' is a versatile, beautiful, and practical choice for gardeners looking to add year-round interest to their landscape.
