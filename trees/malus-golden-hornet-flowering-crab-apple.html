About This Tree

A superb Crab Apple that has been a proven favourite among gardeners in the UK and Ireland for well over half a centuary. 'Golden Hornet' is highly regarded for its brightly coloured, marble sized fruits which provide great interest and colour, often remaining on the tree throughout the winter.

Golden Hornet is a small tree of oval habit, mostly reaching of a height of around 5-8m making it ideal for gardens and spaces of all sizes. The tree begins to produce a floral display during the early spring time when red-pink flowering buds forming, then opening to reveal a dazzling white blossom sure to be enjoyed by all that see it. Come autumn the small fruits will already have formed and can be so prolific in number that the whole tree appears to take on a golden yellow tinge.

This crab apple tree is a very tough performer and will be able to tolerate a large range of soil conditions including clay. Having the tree sheltered will ensure that fruits will continue to hang on the tree even after ripe. Perfect to brighten up any garden. Fully hardy.
