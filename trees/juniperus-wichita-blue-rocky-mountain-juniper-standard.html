About This Tree

Standards

Juniperus 'Wichita Blue' is a captivating evergreen conifer known for its striking silvery-blue foliage that brings a touch of elegance to any landscape. When grown as a standard, this juniper features a distinct tree form with a clear trunk and a rounded or conical crown, creating a sophisticated focal point in gardens or as a statement in container plantings.

Key features of Juniperus 'Wichita Blue' include its dense, needle-like foliage that maintains its vibrant colour year-round, adding visual interest even in the winter months. This juniper is relatively slow-growing, achieving a mature height of approximately 3 to 5 meters and a spread of about 1 to 2 meters, making it an excellent choice for smaller gardens or as an accent tree in larger landscapes.

Notably adaptable, 'Wichita Blue' thrives in a variety of soil types, including sandy, loamy, and clay soils, provided they are well-drained. It prefers full sun to partial shade, with optimal growth and colour development occurring in full sun exposure. Additionally, this juniper is drought-tolerant once established, and it requires minimal maintenance, making it a practical and visually appealing choice for both novice and experienced gardeners. Its resistance to deer and pests further enhances its desirability as a hardy and attractive landscape plant.
