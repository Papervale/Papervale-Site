About This Tree

The Ginkgo biloba 'Menhir', commonly known as the Maidenhair Tree, is a captivating and distinctive ornamental tree. Renowned for its unique, fan-shaped leaves that turn a brilliant yellow in the fall, this cultivar offers a columnar and slender growth habit, making it an excellent choice for narrow spaces or as a striking vertical accent in a landscape design.

Key features of the Ginkgo biloba 'Menhir' include its mature height and spread, which typically reach around 10-12 meters in height and 2-3 meters in spread. This relatively compact size makes it suitable for urban settings, residential gardens, and areas where space is limited but vertical growth is desired. The tree's overall form is erect and tightly columnar, maintaining its shape well without extensive pruning.

In terms of growing conditions, the Maidenhair Tree is remarkably adaptable. It thrives in a wide range of soil types, from sandy to clay, and prefers well-drained soils. It is also highly tolerant of urban pollution, making it a resilient choice for city environments. The 'Menhir' cultivar does well in full sun to partial shade, though it will produce the best autumn colour and overall growth in full sun conditions. Additionally, this tree is known for its resistance to pests and diseases, as well as its impressive longevity, often living for many decades. Its unique aesthetic and hardy nature make the Ginkgo biloba 'Menhir' a standout choice for a variety of landscaping needs.
