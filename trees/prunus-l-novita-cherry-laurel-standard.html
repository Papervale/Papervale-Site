About This Tree

Standard specimens

Prunus laurocerasus 'Novita', commonly known as Cherry Laurel 'Novita', is an evergreen tree that is highly valued for its dense, glossy foliage and robust growth. Grown as a standard, this ornamental tree offers a striking vertical presence, making it an excellent choice for formal gardens, hedging, or as a standalone specimen. One of its most notable features is its lustrous, dark green leaves that can provide year-round interest and a lush backdrop to garden landscapes.

When fully mature, Prunus l. 'Novita' typically reaches a height of about 2.5 to 3.5 meters and has a spread of approximately 2 to 3 meters. This makes it a relatively compact tree suitable for smaller gardens or for creating structured, evergreen screens. In addition to its attractive foliage, 'Novita' produces small, fragrant white flowers in late spring, followed by small, black berries in the autumn, which can attract wildlife such as birds.

Prunus l. 'Novita' is highly adaptable and thrives in a variety of soil types, including clay, loam, and sandy soils, provided they are well-drained. It prefers a position in full sun to partial shade, making it versatile for different garden settings. It is also known for its hardiness and resistance to common pests and diseases. Overall, Cherry Laurel 'Novita' is a low-maintenance tree that offers year-round beauty and structural interest to any landscape.
