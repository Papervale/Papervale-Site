About This Tree

The 'Summerred' apple tree, scientifically known as Malus domestica 'Summerred', is a charming variety celebrated for its early-ripening fruit and exceptional flavour. This deciduous tree belongs to the Rosaceae family and is adorned with beautiful blossoms that precede its delightful apples.

Upon reaching maturity, the 'Summerred' typically stands between 3 to 4 meters tall and boasts a similar spread, creating a rounded and balanced presence in any landscape. The apples produced by this variety are medium-sized, featuring a striking bright red skin that covers a green-yellow base. Their flesh is crisp, juicy, and offers a sweet-tart flavour, making them perfect for both fresh eating and culinary applications. In spring, the tree bursts into bloom with an abundance of pinkish-white flowers, enhancing its ornamental appeal before the fruiting season begins. Harvest time for 'Summerred' apples usually falls between late summer and early autumn, marking it as one of the earlier maturing apple varieties.

This tree thrives in well-drained soils and is adaptable to various soil types, including loamy, sandy, and clay soils, as long as they remain free from waterlogging. For optimal fruit yield and quality, it requires full sun exposure, ideally receiving at least six hours of direct sunlight daily. The 'Summerred' apple tree is an excellent addition to home orchards and gardens, offering a dependable harvest of delicious apples while contributing beauty to the landscape throughout the seasons.
