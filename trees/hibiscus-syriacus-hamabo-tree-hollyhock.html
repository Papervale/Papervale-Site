About This Tree

Hibiscus syriacus 'Hamabo', commonly known as Tree Hollyhock, is a deciduous shrub or small tree that adds a touch of exotic beauty to any garden. It is renowned for its stunning, large, and showy flowers that bloom from mid-summer to early fall. These blossoms are typically pink with a dark red eye, giving them a striking and attractive appearance. The Tree Hollyhock is an excellent choice for gardeners looking for a splash of color during the latter part of the growing season.

Key features of Hibiscus syriacus 'Hamabo' include its mature height and spread. This shrub can grow to a height of approximately 2 to 4 meters and has a spread of about 1.5 to 2.5 meters, making it suitable for both small and large gardens. The Tree Hollyhock has a moderate growth rate and, with proper care, can form a dense, upright, and vase-shaped habit. The leaves are medium to dark green, adding to the plant's lush appearance during the growing season before they drop in the fall.

Tree Hollyhock thrives in well-drained soil and can adapt to a variety of soil types, including sandy, loamy, or clay soils. It prefers a location with full sun to partial shade, as this will promote the best flowering. This hibiscus is relatively low maintenance, with some tolerance to drought once established, though it will perform best with regular watering during dry periods. It is also resistant to most pests and diseases, making it a reliable and robust addition to any landscape. With its eye-catching flowers and ease of care, Hibiscus syriacus 'Hamabo' is a charming and valuable plant for any garden enthusiast.
