About This Tree

Standard

Juniperus scopulorum 'Skyrocket', commonly referred to as the Skyrocket Juniper, is a striking evergreen conifer known for its narrow, columnar growth habit. When grown as a standard, this tree maintains its tall, slender form, making it an excellent choice for garden focal points or for creating vertical interest in landscape designs. Its foliage is a dense, silver-blue colour, which retains its vibrancy throughout the year, providing year-round visual appeal.

Key features of the Skyrocket Juniper include its impressive mature height and relatively narrow spread. Typically, this tree reaches heights of 5 to 7 meters while maintaining a spread of only 0.5 to 1 meter, making it ideal for tight spaces or as a natural privacy screen. The Skyrocket Juniper is also known for its hardiness and adaptability, thriving in a range of soil types from sandy to clay, as long as the soil is well-drained. It prefers full sun but can tolerate partial shade, although its growth may be less vigorous in shadier conditions.

Notable for its low maintenance requirements, the Skyrocket Juniper is drought-tolerant once established and requires minimal pruning to maintain its shape. It is also resistant to many common pests and diseases, making it a reliable choice for both novice and experienced gardeners. This tree's unique form and striking colour make it a standout addition to any landscape, whether used as a specimen plant or in group plantings to create a dramatic effect.
