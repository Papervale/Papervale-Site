About This Tree

The Acer freemanii 'Autumn Blaze' ('Jeffersred'), frequently known as widely celebrated for its spectacular autumn colour, is a hybrid maple that has captivated the hearts of landscapers and garden enthusiasts alike. A testament to the beauty of nature's palette, this tree is renowned for its vibrant red foliage that ignites the landscape with its fiery hues during the autumn season. The 'Autumn Blaze' combines the best attributes of its parent trees, the red maple (Acer rubrum) and the silver maple (Acer saccharinum), resulting in a fast-growing, hardy specimen with a broadly oval to rounded crown.

Reaching a mature height of approximately 15 to 18 meters (50 to 60 feet) and a spread of around 12 meters (40 feet), this majestic tree provides ample shade, making it an excellent choice for wide open spaces, parks, and large backyards. Its ascent to maturity is marked by a moderately fast growth rate, allowing it to quickly establish its presence in a landscape setting.

Apart from its breathtaking autumnal display, the 'Autumn Blaze' maple is not fussy when it comes to soil types, showing a preference for moist, well-drained soils but demonstrating adaptability to a wide range of soil conditions. It thrives in full sun to partial shade, showcasing its versatility and ease of care. This resilience, coupled with its minimal requirement for maintenance, makes it an attractive option for both seasoned gardeners and novices alike. Whether planted as a standalone specimen or as part of a larger arboreal collection, the Acer freemanii 'Autumn Blaze' is a magnificent choice for anyone looking to add a splash of colour and elegance to their outdoor space.
