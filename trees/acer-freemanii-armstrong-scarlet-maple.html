About This Tree

The Acer freemanii 'Armstrong' more commonly known as the Scarlet Maple is a captivating variety of maple known for its slender, upright growth habit, making it an exceptional choice for landscapes seeking vertical accentuation without the extensive spread of other maples. This cultivar reaches a mature height of approximately 15 to 18 meters, with a relatively narrow spread of about 4 to 6 meters, distinguishing it as a remarkably columnar tree suitable for more confined spaces or as a striking street tree.

Key features of the 'Armstrong' maple include its fiery fall foliage, which presents an eye-catching display of reds and oranges, adding a spectacular burst of colour to any garden. During spring and summer, its leaves are a fresh, vibrant green, which contrasts beautifully against its smooth, silver-grey bark. The Acer freemanii 'Armstrong' is also notable for its adaptability to a wide range of soil types, though it prefers moist, well-drained soils for optimal growth. In terms of sun and shade preferences, this tree thrives in full sun to partial shade, making it versatile for different landscape designs and exposures.

Another commendable feature of the 'Armstrong' maple is its relative resistance to pests and diseases, making it a resilient choice for gardeners seeking a low maintenance yet visually stunning tree. Its non-invasive root system is another benefit, reducing concerns about planting near sidewalks, driveways, or buildings. Whether planted as a solitary specimen for its architectural qualities or used in a row for screening or avenue planting, the Acer freemanii 'Armstrong' is an attractive, durable tree that adds year-round interest and elegance to any setting.
