About This Tree

Thujopsis dolabrata, known as the Hiba Arborvitae, is a captivating coniferous tree native to Japan, widely appreciated for its lush, dense foliage. This evergreen species features distinctive, flattened sprays of dark green leaves that exude a leathery texture. The arrangement of the leaves in opposite pairs provides the tree with a unique and appealing appearance throughout the year, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

When it comes to size, the Hiba Arborvitae can reach a mature height ranging from approximately 6 to 18 meters (20 to 60 feet), depending on the growing conditions and level of care it receives. The tree's spread is also notable, extending about 3 to 6 meters (10 to 20 feet), which makes it a substantial and eye-catching presence in any landscape setting.

The Thujopsis dolabrata boasts several notable features. Its glossy, deep green foliage is a standout characteristic, maintaining its vibrant colour all year long. When the leaves are crushed, they emit a pleasant fragrance, enhancing the tree's sensory appeal. The bark of the Hiba Arborvitae is another feature of interest; its reddish-brown hue and tendency to peel in narrow strips add a layer of texture and visual interest. Additionally, the tree produces small, oval cones, which serve as a subtle decorative element.

In terms of growing conditions, Thujopsis dolabrata thrives best in well-drained, slightly acidic soils. However, it is adaptable to a variety of soil types as long as they are not waterlogged. The tree prefers locations with partial shade to full sun. In warmer climates, it benefits from some protection against harsh afternoon sunlight. Planting it in a spot where it receives morning sun and afternoon shade can help preserve its vibrant foliage, especially in areas with hot summers.

This species is relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal pruning and care once established. Its hardiness and attractive features make it an excellent choice for both novice and experienced gardeners alike, offering beauty and resilience with minimal effort.
