About This Tree

The Silver Wattle, also known as the Mimosa or the Acacia dealbata is an evergreen tree from Australia and has become popular in Europe because of its grand floral display.

Mimosa can be seen year-round with a magnificent display of green foliage which features fern like leaves that are around 12cms in length. During the spring months, bright yellow blowers bloom on the Mimosa which contrast very well to the deep green leaves giving a spectacular display on the tree which will look amazing in any garden the entire way during the summer months.

The Mimosa will grow to a small height of around 3-7m when mature, making it perfect for small gardens.
Silver Wattle will perform best in well drained soils and will perform well in acidic, alkaline and also chalky soil making it very easy for anyone to grow.
