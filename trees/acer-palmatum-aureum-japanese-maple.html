About This Tree

Acer palmatum 'Aureum', commonly known as the Golden Full Moon Maple, is a captivating deciduous tree renowned for its stunning foliage and elegant form. This small to medium-sized tree typically reaches a mature height of around 4 to 6 meters, with a similar spread, making it an excellent choice for gardens with limited space or as a striking accent in larger landscapes.

One of the key features of 'Aureum' is its distinctive, palmate leaves, which unfurl in spring with a bright yellow to chartreuse hue, providing a dazzling display of color. As the seasons progress, the leaves may develop subtle tinges of orange or red, particularly in autumn, before they fall, adding seasonal interest. The delicate, finely cut leaves create a lacy texture that is both elegant and visually appealing.

'Aureum' prefers well-drained, slightly acidic soil and thrives best in partial shade to protect its delicate foliage from scorching under intense midday sun. It can tolerate full sun in cooler climates, provided it receives adequate moisture. This maple is relatively low-maintenance, requiring only minimal pruning to maintain its shape and remove any dead or damaged branches. Its vibrant coloration and graceful form make Acer palmatum 'Aureum' a standout specimen in any garden, offering year-round beauty and interest.
