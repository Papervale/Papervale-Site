About This Tree

The Sorbus 'Autumn Spire', also known as Rowan, is a deciduous tree renowned for its striking autumnal foliage and compact, upright growth habit. This elegant tree is a perfect choice for small gardens or urban settings where space is at a premium. It typically reaches a mature height of about 4 to 6 meters, with a spread of 1.5 to 2 meters, making it ideal for narrow spaces or as a focal point in a landscaped area.

One of the standout features of the Sorbus 'Autumn Spire' is its vibrant autumn foliage, which transitions to shades of orange, red, and gold, creating a stunning visual display. In spring, the tree produces clusters of creamy white flowers that attract pollinators, followed by small, red-orange berries in late summer to fall. These berries not only add to the tree's ornamental appeal but also provide a valuable food source for birds.

Sorbus 'Autumn Spire' thrives in well-drained soil and can adapt to a variety of soil types, including sandy, loamy, and clay soils. It prefers full sun to partial shade, ensuring it receives at least a few hours of direct sunlight each day to promote healthy growth and vibrant foliage. This tree is relatively low-maintenance and resistant to many common pests and diseases, making it an excellent addition to any garden. Its compact size and stunning seasonal changes make it a versatile and visually appealing choice for gardeners and landscapers alike.
