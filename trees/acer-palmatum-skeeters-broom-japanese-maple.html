About This Tree

ACER palmatum 'Skeeter's Broom' / Japanese Maple

Acer palmatum 'Skeeter's Broom', commonly known as Skeeter's Broom Japanese Maple, is a captivating deciduous shrub or small tree that boasts a distinctively compact and upright form. This cultivar is particularly cherished for its striking foliage and manageable size, making it a popular choice for small gardens or container growing. The deeply lobed leaves emerge a vibrant crimson in the spring, transitioning to a rich burgundy during the summer, and culminating in a spectacular display of fiery red in the autumn.

At maturity, 'Skeeter's Broom' typically reaches a height of 2-3 meters with a similar spread, forming a neat and tidy silhouette that adds structure and colour to any landscape. The tree's growth habit is generally more upright and columnar compared to other Japanese Maples, making it an excellent choice for narrow spaces or as a vertical accent in mixed borders.

'Skeeter's Broom' thrives best in well-drained, slightly acidic to neutral soil. It prefers a location with partial shade to full sun; however, in hotter climates, some afternoon shade can help prevent leaf scorch. Adequate moisture, especially during dry periods, is essential to maintain its vibrant foliage. This stunning cultivar is not only an ornamental gem but also relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal pruning and care once established. With its dazzling seasonal colour changes and compact form, Acer palmatum 'Skeeter's Broom' is a standout addition to any garden.
