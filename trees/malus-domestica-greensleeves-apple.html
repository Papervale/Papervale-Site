About This Tree

Malus domestica 'Greensleeves' is a delightful cultivar of the domesticated apple, known for its distinctively crisp and juicy fruit. This apple tree typically reaches a mature height of around 3 to 4 meters, making it an excellent choice for small gardens or orchard spaces. The spread of the tree is similar, extending to about 3 to 4 meters, thus ensuring a balanced and manageable growth pattern.

One of the key features of 'Greensleeves' is its vibrant green apples, which are not only visually appealing but also offer a refreshing flavour profile that is both sweet and slightly tangy. These apples are usually harvested in late summer to early autumn, providing a bountiful yield that can be enjoyed fresh or used in various culinary applications.

Another notable aspect of 'Greensleeves' is its adaptability to different soil types. It thrives in well-drained, fertile soils but can also tolerate a range of other soil conditions if there is adequate drainage. The tree prefers a sunny position to maximize fruit production, although it can tolerate partial shade. Regular watering and occasional feeding with a balanced fertilizer will ensure optimal growth and fruiting. This cultivar is also known for its resistance to common apple diseases, making it a robust and low-maintenance choice for gardeners and orchardists alike.
