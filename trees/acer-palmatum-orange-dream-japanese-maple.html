About This Tree

Acer palmatum 'Orange Dream', commonly known as the Japanese Maple 'Orange Dream', is a captivating deciduous tree that is highly prized for its stunning foliage and elegant form. This variety is particularly renowned for its vibrant, ever-changing leaf colour throughout the growing season. In early spring, the lobed leaves emerge in a brilliant yellow-orange hue, gradually transitioning to a lush green in the summer, before ending the season with a display of gold and orange in Autumn. The delicate leaves and graceful branching create a striking presence in any garden.

Reaching a mature height of approximately 3 to 4 meters and a spread of about 2 to 3 meters, 'Orange Dream' maintains a compact and upright growth habit, making it an excellent choice for smaller gardens, containers, or as a focal point in landscape design. Its relatively slow growth rate and manageable size also make it suitable for bonsai cultivation. The tree's ornamental value is further enhanced by its smooth, grey bark that provides winter interest when the tree is bare of leaves.

'Orange Dream' thrives best in well-drained, slightly acidic soil that is rich in organic matter. It prefers partial shade to full sun, although in hotter climates, some afternoon shade is beneficial to prevent leaf scorch. Adequate moisture levels should be maintained, particularly during dry spells, to ensure the health and vibrancy of the foliage. This versatile and low-maintenance tree is a favourite among gardeners and landscapers for its year-round beauty and adaptability to various garden settings.
