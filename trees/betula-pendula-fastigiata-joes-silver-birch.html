About This Tree

The Betula pendula 'Fastigiata Joes', commonly known as the Silver Birch, is a striking deciduous tree renowned for its elegant, narrow pyramidal form. This cultivar is particularly appreciated for its upright growth habit, making it an excellent choice for smaller gardens or urban settings where space can be limited. The tree is characterized by its distinctive white bark that exfoliates in papery strips, providing year-round visual interest. Its leaves are diamond-shaped and turn a lovely yellow in the autumn, adding seasonal colour to any landscape.

At maturity, 'Fastigiata Joes' typically reaches a height of around 10-15 meters, with a spread of approximately 3-5 meters. This relatively compact size compared to other birches makes it suitable for planting in tighter spaces while still offering the vertical element that birches are known for. The tree produces small, inconspicuous flowers in the spring, which are followed by tiny nutlets in the autumn, providing a food source for various bird species.

Betula pendula 'Fastigiata Joes' thrives best in well-drained, loamy, or sandy soils and can tolerate a range of soil pH levels from acidic to neutral. It prefers full sun but can also tolerate partial shade. This tree is relatively low-maintenance and is resistant to many pests and diseases, making it a resilient choice for both novice and experienced gardeners. Its hardiness and adaptability, combined with its striking appearance, make the Silver Birch 'Fastigiata Joes' a beloved addition to many landscapes.
