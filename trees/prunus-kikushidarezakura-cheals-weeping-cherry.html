About This Tree

Certainly the most popular weeping cherry available and rightly so, Cheals Weeping brings springtime cheer and beautiful blossom to any space it occupies.

This cherry however has much more to offer than just its spring flowers. The foliage when it first emerges has deep bronze tones and again in later in the year has good autumn colour. The architectural shape and weeping habit of Cheals Weeping that give it appeal and natural beauty throughout the year. Its on these arching branches that the double pink flowers are produced in dense clusters during spring

The perfect tree for many applications; a small garden, centre of the lawn or even for growing in a pot in confined spaces. Grows well on any reasonable soil and is fully hardy.

Received the Royal Horticultural Society's Award of Garden Merit in 1984 & 2002
