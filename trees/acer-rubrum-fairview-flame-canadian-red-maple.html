About This Tree

The Acer rubrum 'Fairview Flame,' also known as the Canadian Red Maple, is a striking deciduous tree renowned for its vibrant autumn foliage. This cultivar is particularly admired for its brilliant red leaves that create a fiery display in the fall, making it a favourite among landscape designers and homeowners alike. In spring, the tree produces small red flowers that add an early splash of colour before the leaves fully emerge. Throughout the summer, its green, lobed leaves provide a lush canopy, which transitions to its famous red hues as the temperatures drop.

At maturity, the 'Fairview Flame' can reach an impressive height of 12-15 meters with a spread of about 10-12 meters. This makes it an excellent choice for providing shade and adding vertical interest to large gardens or parks. The tree has a relatively fast growth rate and a strong, upright branching habit, contributing to its robust and stately appearance.

The Canadian Red Maple thrives in a variety of soil types, including acidic, loamy, moist, sandy, silty loam, and well-drained soils. It is quite adaptable but performs best in well-drained, slightly acidic soil. This tree prefers full sun to partial shade, requiring at least 4-6 hours of direct sunlight daily to develop its best colour and form. Additionally, it is relatively low-maintenance and tolerant of urban conditions, making it suitable for planting in a range of environments from rural landscapes to city parks. Overall, the Acer rubrum 'Fairview Flame' is a versatile and visually stunning choice for any landscape project.
