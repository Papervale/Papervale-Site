About This Tree

Prunus l. 'Etna', commonly known as the Etna Cherry Laurel, is a robust and versatile evergreen shrub or small tree that is particularly prized for its dense and bushy habit. This cultivar is known for its attractive, glossy, dark green foliage which remains vibrant throughout the year. New growth often emerges with a striking bronze-red hue, adding seasonal interest to any landscape. This feature makes it an excellent choice for hedging or screening purposes, as it provides year-round privacy and aesthetic appeal.

When grown to maturity, Prunus l. 'Etna' can reach a height of approximately 2.5 to 3 meters, with a similar spread. Its compact form and symmetrical growth make it a suitable option for both large gardens and smaller spaces where a structured appearance is desired. The tree produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring, which are followed by red berries that gradually darken to black. These berries are a source of food for birds, enhancing the ecological value of the plant.

Prunus l. 'Etna' thrives in well-drained soils, preferring slightly acidic to neutral conditions, though it is adaptable to a range of soil types. It performs best in full sun to partial shade, making it a flexible choice for various garden settings. This cultivar is also noted for its resilience to challenging urban conditions, including pollution and poor air quality, making it an ideal choice for city gardens. Its low maintenance requirements and resistance to common pests and diseases further contribute to its popularity among gardeners and landscapers.
