About This Tree

Ilex 'Nellie R. Stevens', commonly known as Nellie Stevens Holly, is a versatile and robust evergreen tree that stands out for its dense, pyramidal form and dark green foliage. This hybrid holly showcases glossy, spiny leaves that provide year-round interest, making it an excellent choice for hedges, privacy screens, or as a standalone specimen. One of its most eye-catching features is the profusion of bright red berries that appear in autumn and persist through winter, adding a splash of colour to the landscape and attracting birds.

At maturity, Nellie Stevens Holly typically reaches a height of 6 to 9 meters with a spread of 3 to 4.5 meters. Its growth rate is moderate to fast, making it a suitable option for gardeners looking for a relatively quick-growing evergreen. The tree prefers well-drained, slightly acidic to neutral soil but is highly adaptable to a range of soil types, including clay, loam, and sandy soils.

In terms of light requirements, Nellie Stevens Holly thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is known for its resilience and can tolerate a variety of growing conditions, including drought and urban pollution, once established. This holly variety is also relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal pruning to maintain its shape. With its combination of aesthetic appeal, adaptability, and ease of care, Ilex 'Nellie R. Stevens' is a popular choice for both residential and commercial landscapes.
