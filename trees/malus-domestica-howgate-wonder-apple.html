About This Tree

Malus domestica 'Howgate Wonder' is a vigorous and robust apple tree variety known for its impressive fruit size and excellent culinary qualities. This deciduous tree typically reaches a mature height of around 4.5 to 6 meters, with a similar spread, making it a substantial addition to any orchard or garden. Its wide, rounded canopy provides ample shade and visual appeal through the seasons.

Key features of 'Howgate Wonder' include its large, greenish-yellow apples, which can weigh up to 900 grams each. These apples have a firm, crisp texture and a balanced sweet-tart flavour, making them perfect for baking, cooking, and even storing for extended periods. The tree blossoms in mid-spring, producing beautiful white to pinkish flowers that not only attract pollinators but also contribute to the tree's ornamental value.

'Howgate Wonder' thrives in well-drained, loamy soils but is adaptable to a range of soil types, including sandy and clay soils, provided they are not waterlogged. The tree prefers full sun to ensure optimal fruit production, though it can tolerate partial shade. It is relatively hardy and disease-resistant, making it a reliable choice for both amateur and experienced gardeners. Regular pruning will help maintain its shape and encourage better fruit yield, ensuring that the 'Howgate Wonder' remains a fruitful and attractive feature in your garden for many years to come.
