About This Tree

MALUS domestica 'Red Devil' / Apple

The Malus domestica 'Red Devil' is a captivating apple tree variety known for its striking appearance and robust fruit production. This deciduous tree is a cultivar of the common apple and is particularly appreciated for its deep, red-skinned apples, which are both visually appealing and delicious. The apples have a crisp texture and a sweet-tart flavour, making them ideal for fresh eating, juicing, and cooking.

Key features of the 'Red Devil' apple tree include its vibrant red fruits, which are ready for harvest in late summer to early autumn. The tree itself is relatively compact, typically reaching a mature height of about 3 to 4 meters and a spread of approximately 2.5 to 3 meters. This makes it an excellent choice for smaller gardens or for those who wish to grow fruit trees in limited spaces. The tree also produces beautiful white blossoms with a hint of pink in the spring, which are not only attractive but also beneficial for pollinators like bees.

In terms of growing conditions, the 'Red Devil' apple tree prefers well-drained, fertile soil with a pH range of 6.0 to 7.0. It thrives in full sun, which ensures the best fruit production and quality. While it can tolerate partial shade, the yield and taste of the apples may be affected. Additionally, this cultivar is relatively disease-resistant, making it easier to maintain compared to some other apple varieties. Whether you are an experienced gardener or a novice, the Malus domestica 'Red Devil' is a delightful and rewarding addition to any orchard or garden.
