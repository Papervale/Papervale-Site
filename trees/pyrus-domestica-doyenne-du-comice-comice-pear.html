About This Tree

Pyrus domestica 'Doyenne du Comice,' commonly known as the Comice pear, is a deciduous fruit tree celebrated for its exceptional fruit quality and ornamental value. This cultivar is revered for producing large, juicy, and sweet pears that are often considered some of the finest dessert pears available. The tree typically reaches a mature height of 4 to 5 meters and a spread of about 3 to 4 meters, making it an excellent choice for both small and medium-sized gardens.

The Comice pear tree is characterized by its attractive, glossy green foliage that turns a beautiful yellow in the fall, providing seasonal interest. In spring, the tree is adorned with clusters of white blossoms, which not only add a charming aesthetic but also attract a variety of pollinators, essential for fruit set. The pears themselves are typically harvested in late autumn and have a distinctive, rounded shape with a slightly russeted surface.

For optimal growth and fruit production, Pyrus domestica 'Doyenne du Comice' prefers well-drained, loamy soils but can tolerate a range of soil types as long as they are not waterlogged. It thrives in full sun, which ensures the best fruit quality and ripening. While it can tolerate partial shade, full sunlight is crucial for maximum yield and flavour development. Regular pruning is recommended to maintain its shape and health, and to encourage better air circulation, which helps prevent disease. Overall, the Comice pear tree is a delightful addition to any garden, offering both aesthetic beauty and delicious fruit.
