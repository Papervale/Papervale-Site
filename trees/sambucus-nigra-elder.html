About This Tree

Sambucus nigra is a bushy large shrub or small tree, with pinnate leaves turning pale yellow in autumn, flat sprays of fragrant cream flowers in early summer followed by small black berries. Growing capabilities of 4-8m tall with an ultimate spread of approx. 2.5–4m wide.

A perfect tree for any type of garden which can be easily grown with its beautiful flowers, green foliage and its berries. Grows best in not too dry soil in sun or part shade and will be able to tolerate any soil type including chalk.
