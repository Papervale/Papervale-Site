About This Tree

The Prunus serrulata 'Shimidsu', commonly known as Miyako, is an exquisite ornamental flowering cherry tree celebrated for its breathtaking beauty and delicate blossoms. This tree is highly favoured in gardens and landscapes due to its striking floral display that signals the arrival of spring. The 'Shimidsu' produces large, double, pale pink blossoms, transforming the tree into a vibrant spectacle during its blooming season. These fragrant flowers not only enhance the visual appeal but also attract bees and other beneficial pollinators, contributing to the ecosystem.

Beyond its captivating flowers, the Prunus serrulata 'Shimidsu' boasts lush, glossy green foliage that creates a rich backdrop for its blossoms. As autumn approaches, the leaves shift to a stunning array of reds and oranges, adding a dynamic splash of colour to any landscape. The tree's smooth, grey-brown bark offers an elegant structural element, providing year-round interest even when the tree is not in bloom. Reaching a mature height and spread of approximately 5 to 6 meters, the 'Shimidsu' forms a graceful, rounded canopy that fits well in a variety of garden sizes.

This tree has a moderate growth rate, making it manageable for most gardeners. It thrives in well-drained soil, with a preference for soil rich in organic matter that retains moisture without becoming waterlogged. The 'Shimidsu' performs best in full sun to partial shade, where ample sunlight promotes robust flowering and healthy foliage development. Maintenance is straightforward, with pruning recommended during the dormant season to maintain its shape and remove any dead or diseased wood. With its breathtaking blossoms and seasonal foliage changes, the Prunus serrulata 'Shimidsu' (Miyako) is a versatile and aesthetically pleasing centrepiece for any garden.
