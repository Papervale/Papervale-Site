About This Tree

The PRUNUS 'Sunset Boulevard,' commonly known as the White Flowering Cherry, is a stunning ornamental tree that graces gardens and landscapes with its remarkable beauty. This deciduous tree is renowned for its abundant springtime display of pure white flowers, which blanket the branches in a breathtaking spectacle. The blossoms typically appear in early to mid-spring, creating a picturesque scene that attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies. As the seasons progress, the tree's lush green foliage turns to vibrant shades of yellow, orange, and red in the fall, offering a magnificent display of autumn colours.

Key features of the PRUNUS 'Sunset Boulevard' include its moderate growth rate and a mature height ranging between 5 to 8 meters. The tree's spread is equally impressive, reaching about 4 to 7 meters, making it an excellent choice for medium to large gardens. Its rounded, symmetrical canopy provides a pleasing structural element to any landscape design.

In terms of growing conditions, the White Flowering Cherry thrives in well-drained soils and prefers a location that receives full sun to partial shade. It is adaptable to a variety of soil types, including loam, clay, and sand, as long as the soil is not excessively wet. This tree is relatively low-maintenance and resistant to many common diseases, although it can benefit from regular pruning to maintain its shape and remove any dead or diseased branches. Overall, PRUNUS 'Sunset Boulevard' is a versatile and elegant tree that adds year-round interest and charm to any outdoor space.
