About This Tree

The Malus domestica 'Spartan' is a charming and versatile apple tree that is highly valued for its attractive, medium-sized fruits and robust hardiness. This deciduous tree typically reaches a mature height of around 4 to 5 meters, with a spread of approximately 3 to 4 meters, making it well-suited for smaller gardens or orchard settings. The 'Spartan' variety is particularly prized for its glossy, deep red apples that offer a crisp and sweet flavour, ideal for both fresh eating and culinary uses.

One of the key features of the 'Spartan' apple tree is its vigorous growth and reliable fruit production. It blooms in mid to late spring, showcasing clusters of fragrant white to pale pink flowers that are not only visually appealing but also attract pollinators such as bees. The tree's green foliage provides a lush backdrop throughout the growing season, transitioning to a beautiful display of autumn colours before shedding its leaves in preparation for winter.

When it comes to growing conditions, the 'Spartan' apple tree thrives best in well-draining, loamy soils with a slightly acidic to neutral pH. It prefers full sun exposure, which ensures optimal fruit development and overall health. While this tree is relatively low-maintenance, it benefits from regular pruning to maintain its shape and encourage better air circulation, thereby reducing the risk of disease. With its compact size and delightful fruit, the Malus domestica 'Spartan' is an excellent addition to any garden or orchard, providing both aesthetic beauty and delicious harvests.
