About This Tree

Juniperus scopulorum 'Blue Arrow' is a striking narrow columnar evergreen tree that belongs to the cypress family. It is known for its vibrant blue-green foliage that retains its color year-round, making it a standout in any landscape. The tree typically grows to a mature height of around 12-15 feet with a spread of about 2-3 feet, making it perfect for tight spaces or as a focal point in a garden.

One of the key features of the 'Blue Arrow' Juniper is its adaptability to various soil types, including well-drained soils and even rocky or sandy soils, making it a versatile choice for different garden settings. Its drought-tolerant nature and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for landscaping projects.

Overall, JUNIPERUS scopulorum 'Blue Arrow' is a fantastic choice for those looking to add a touch of elegance and structure to their outdoor space. With its unique appearance, low maintenance requirements, and adaptability to different soil conditions, this tree is sure to be a standout feature in any landscape.
