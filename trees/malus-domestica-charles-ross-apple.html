About This Tree

Malus domestica 'Charles Ross' is a delightful apple tree that stands out for its versatility, producing fruit that is perfect for both fresh consumption and cooking. The apples are large and visually appealing, featuring a red-orange flush against a yellow background, and are celebrated for their sweet, aromatic flavour, making them a favourite among both gardeners and culinary enthusiasts. The tree typically reaches a mature height of around 4 to 5 meters, with a similar spread, making it an ideal option for smaller gardens or as part of a mixed orchard.

In spring, 'Charles Ross' enchants with a profusion of delicate white blossoms, tinged with pink, creating a stunning display that attracts pollinators. The apples are generally ready for harvest from late September to October and are known for their excellent storage capabilities, allowing for enjoyment throughout the winter months. The vibrant green foliage adds a lush backdrop, enhancing the overall aesthetic of the tree.

This variety thrives best in well-drained, fertile soil, favouring slightly acidic to neutral conditions but demonstrating adaptability to a range of soil types as long as drainage is adequate. 'Charles Ross' prefers full sun to maximize fruit production and flavour, although it can tolerate partial shade, which may result in reduced yield and quality. Overall, Malus domestica 'Charles Ross' is a wonderful addition to any garden, combining ornamental beauty with culinary rewards.
