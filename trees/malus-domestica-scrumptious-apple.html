About This Tree

The Malus domestica 'Scrumptious', commonly referred to as the Scrumptious Apple Tree, stands out as a remarkable fruit-bearing tree celebrated for its delightful and fragrant apples. This particular cultivar is a favourite among gardeners due to its early harvesting capabilities and adaptability to various climates. The Scrumptious Apple produces medium-sized, red-blushed apples that are sweet, crisp, and juicy, boasting an excellent flavour balance that makes them perfect for both fresh consumption and culinary applications. In spring, the tree enchants with a stunning display of fragrant blossoms in shades of white to pale pink, which attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

Typically, the apples begin to ripen from late summer to early autumn, allowing for an extended harvest season. When fully mature, the Scrumptious Apple Tree reaches a height of approximately 3 to 4 meters and can spread up to 3 meters wide, making it a well-suited choice for smaller gardens. This tree thrives best in well-drained, loamy soils that are rich in organic matter, and it can adapt to a range of soil pH levels from slightly acidic to neutral. Preferring sunny locations, the Scrumptious Apple Tree requires at least 6 to 8 hours of direct sunlight daily to ensure optimal fruit production and flavour development. Additionally, it is well-suited for temperate climates and can tolerate light frosts, making it a reliable option for many regions. Notably, this cultivar is recognized for its resistance to common apple diseases such as scab and mildew, which minimizes the need for frequent chemical treatments. Overall, the Malus domestica 'Scrumptious' is an excellent selection for gardeners looking for a hardy, early-harvest apple tree that offers exceptional fruit quality and adaptability.
