About This Tree

Abies Nobilis (Noble Fir) A tall and slender tree with a narrow conical crown. The Noble Fir can grow up to between 40-70 meters in height yet the growth rate is very slow. The needle-like leaves are a grey/green with narrow silver bands on the underside. A very popular Christmas tree due to its high needle retention rate.
