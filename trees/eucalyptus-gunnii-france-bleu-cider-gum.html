About This Tree

Eucalyptus gunnii 'France Bleu', commonly known as Cider Gum, is a remarkable evergreen tree celebrated for its eye-catching foliage and adaptability to various garden settings. This cultivar is particularly appreciated for its compact growth habit and striking silver-blue leaves that maintain their vibrant hue throughout the year. As the tree matures, its juvenile leaves transition to a more elongated, glaucous form, adding a unique texture to the landscape. Typically, Eucalyptus gunnii 'France Bleu' reaches a height of about 4 to 6 meters and spreads around 3 to 4 meters, making it an excellent choice for smaller gardens or as a focal point in larger ones.

One of the notable features of this tree is its smooth bark, which exfoliates in strips, revealing a multi-hued surface that enhances its visual appeal throughout the seasons. The growth rate of this tree is moderate to fast, allowing it to establish quickly and fill out beautifully in any garden setting. Additionally, it is hardy in most climates, though it may require some protection in extremely cold areas. Eucalyptus gunnii 'France Bleu' thrives in well-drained soils and is adaptable to various soil types, tolerating both acidic and neutral pH levels. It prefers full sun to achieve optimal growth and maintain its vibrant foliage, although it can tolerate partial shade. Overall, this tree is an excellent choice for gardeners seeking to introduce a splash of colour and texture to their gardens, thanks to its beautiful foliage and manageable size.
