About This Tree

The Malus 'Royalty', commonly known as the Flowering Crab Apple, is a stunning ornamental tree known for its vibrant and showy appearance. Key features of this tree include its deep red to purple foliage that provides a striking contrast in any garden or landscape. In the spring, the tree bursts into bloom with clusters of fragrant, dark pink to red flowers, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. By autumn, the tree produces small, glossy, dark red crab apples that add further visual interest and can provide food for wildlife.

At maturity, the Malus 'Royalty' typically reaches a height of 4-5 meters, with a spread of approximately 3-4 meters, making it a medium-sized tree suitable for a variety of garden settings. Its compact size and rounded crown make it an excellent choice for small gardens, urban landscapes, or as a focal point in larger spaces.

The Flowering Crab Apple thrives in well-drained, loamy soil but is adaptable to a range of soil types, including sandy and clay soils, as long as they are not excessively wet. It prefers full sun to partial shade, which ensures the best foliage colour and flower production. Notably, this tree is relatively low-maintenance, resistant to many common pests and diseases, and can tolerate some drought once established. The Malus 'Royalty' is a superb addition to any landscape, offering year-round beauty and ecological benefits.
