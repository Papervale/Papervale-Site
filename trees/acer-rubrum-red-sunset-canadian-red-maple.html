About This Tree

The Acer rubrum 'Red Sunset', commonly known as the Canadian Red Maple, is a captivating deciduous tree celebrated for its vibrant fall foliage. This cultivar is particularly prized for its brilliant red leaves that transform landscapes into a spectacular autumnal display. A medium to fast-growing tree, the Red Sunset typically reaches a mature height of approximately 12 to 18 meters with a spread of around 10 to 15 meters, making it an excellent choice for providing shade and enhancing large garden spaces.

Key features of the Red Sunset Maple include its pyramidal to rounded crown and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, from sandy to clay, as long as the soil is well-drained. It prefers full sun to partial shade, requiring at least six hours of direct sunlight daily to maintain its robust growth and vibrant leaf colour. The tree also produces small red flowers in early spring, adding an additional layer of seasonal interest before the leaves fully emerge.

Notably, the Acer rubrum 'Red Sunset' is tolerant of urban conditions, including pollution and compacted soil, making it a popular choice for city planting and public parks. Its adaptability to different environments and relatively low maintenance requirements further enhances its appeal. This versatile tree not only offers aesthetic beauty but also contributes to ecological health by providing habitat for wildlife and improving air quality.
