About This Tree

Acer palmatum ‘Fireglow’ is a captivating variety of the Japanese Maple, renowned for its stunning foliage and elegant structure. This deciduous tree is particularly admired for its deeply lobed leaves, which emerge in a brilliant scarlet-red hue during spring and gradually shift to a rich burgundy as the season advances. By autumn, the foliage transforms into a vibrant crimson, offering a breathtaking visual display that enhances any garden. As a dwarf variety, the 'Fireglow' typically reaches a mature height and spread of about 3 to 4 meters, making it an ideal choice for smaller gardens, patios, or as an eye-catching centrepiece within larger landscapes.

One of the standout features of the 'Fireglow' is its vivid red leaves, which ensure year-round interest. Its upright, vase-shaped growth habit contributes vertical appeal to garden spaces, while the smooth, grey bark adds subtle charm during the winter months. In terms of cultivation, the Acer palmatum ‘Fireglow’ thrives in well-drained, slightly acidic to neutral soil, preferring rich, organic matter for optimal growth. It flourishes in full sun to partial shade, though some protection from the intense afternoon sun is advisable in hotter climates to prevent leaf scorch. Regular watering is crucial, especially during dry spells, as the tree has a shallow root system that necessitates consistent moisture to thrive.
