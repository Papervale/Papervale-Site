About This Tree

The Magnolia 'Galaxy' is a striking deciduous tree that enchants gardeners with its impressive display of large, tulip-shaped flowers. Grown as a standard, Magnolia 'Galaxy' exhibits a single, straight trunk with a well-defined canopy that provides an elegant and architectural presence in any landscape. This variety is particularly cherished for its profusion of rosy-pink blooms that emerge in early to mid-spring, filling the air with a subtle, sweet fragrance. The flowers can reach up to 25 centimetres in diameter, making an eye-catching addition to gardens and parks alike.

At maturity, Magnolia 'Galaxy' typically reaches a height of about 7.5 to 9 meters with a spread of approximately 4.5 to 6 meters (15 to 20 feet). Its upright and pyramidal growth habit makes it an excellent choice for a focal point in garden design. The tree's dark green leaves provide a lush backdrop for the blossoms and turn a lovely golden-brown in autumn, adding seasonal interest. Additionally, the bark of the Magnolia 'Galaxy' is smooth and gray, providing winter interest when the tree is bare.

Magnolia 'Galaxy' thrives best in well-drained, slightly acidic to neutral soils, and it prefers a location with full sun to partial shade. It is adaptable to a range of soil types but performs exceptionally well in rich, moist soils. While it can tolerate some shade, planting it in a sunny spot will ensure the best flower production. Regular watering, especially during dry periods, and a layer of mulch to retain moisture and regulate temperature around the roots can help maintain its health. Overall, this tree is a magnificent addition to any garden, offering year-round beauty and a touch of elegance.
