About This Tree

The Betula albosinensis 'Fascination', commonly known as the Chinese Red Birch, is an enchanting deciduous tree renowned for its strikingly beautiful bark and elegant form. This tree is celebrated for its smooth, peeling bark that displays a spectrum of colours ranging from coppery-red to creamy-white, providing year-round visual interest. In addition to its captivating bark, the tree exhibits ovate, dark green leaves that turn a vibrant yellow in the autumn, creating a stunning seasonal display.

Mature specimens of the Chinese Red Birch typically reach a height of 10 to 12 meters, with a spread of 6 to 8 meters, making it a moderately sized tree suitable for a variety of landscape applications. Its graceful, upright habit and relatively compact size make it an excellent choice for both larger gardens and smaller urban spaces. The 'Fascination' cultivar is particularly noted for its uniform growth and resistance to common birch diseases, adding to its appeal as a reliable ornamental tree.

In terms of growing conditions, Betula albosinensis 'Fascination' thrives best in well-drained, moist soil types, including loam, clay, and sand. It prefers a position in full sun to partial shade, ensuring optimal growth and bark coloration. This tree is also relatively hardy, tolerating a range of environmental conditions once established. For gardeners and landscapers looking to add a touch of elegance and year-round interest to their spaces, the Chinese Red Birch 'Fascination' offers a blend of beauty, resilience, and adaptability.
