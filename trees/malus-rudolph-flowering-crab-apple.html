About This Tree

There can be few finer sights and scents in any garden than that of an apple tree in full blossom. Malus Rudolph is one of the finest flowering trees with its beauty being in its bright pink flowers appearing during Spring.

Malus Rudolph can be seen displaying a beautiful range of bright pink flowers during spring, contrasting well with the small light green leaves which also start to grow around this time. These flowers slowly are followed by many small elongated fruits which come autumn will grow into large Orange-Red crab apples which will last to the late autumn. Around this time the light green foliage will become a magnificent bronze red-brown colour providing a beautiful display in any garden.

Malus Rudolph is easy to grow and performs well on any well drained garden soil. Rudolph has proven to tolerate air pollution and is ideal for small roadside front gardens. Rudolph is also an excellent pollinator for other fruit trees. A highly recommended tree that can be used in a wide range of gardens and situations bringing brightness and life to each and everyone. Hardy.
