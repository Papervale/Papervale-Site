Delivery

Do you deliver across the UK and Ireland?

Yes. We deliver throughout the United Kingdom and Ireland via a 2–3 day tracked courier service. For customers in Co. Down and surrounding areas we also offer a personal delivery service direct from the nursery.

How long does delivery take?

All orders are dispatched via a 2–3 day tracked courier service. Local customers in Co. Down and surrounding areas can arrange personal delivery direct from the nursery — contact us to discuss timing.

What is included with my tree delivery?

Every tree delivery includes our planting and aftercare guide, so your tree gets off to the best possible start from the moment it arrives. You can also read the full guide online at Planting & Aftercare.

Can I collect my tree directly from the nursery?

Yes, you are welcome to collect directly from our nursery in Rathfriland. We are open Saturday 10am–2pm, and weekday visits are available by appointment. Call us on 028 3085 0059 or email us to arrange a convenient time.

Ordering & Stock

How do I check what trees you currently have in stock?

Download our current availability list — it is updated each season and lists all current stock by species, size, and format. Available as a PDF or Excel spreadsheet. You can also browse the tree catalogue or shop online.

When are bareroot trees available?

Bareroot and rootball trees are available from November through March during the dormant season. Container-grown stock is available all year round. Contact us if you are planning ahead for a specific season.

What if the tree I need is not in your current stock list?

Get in touch — we are often able to source specific stock on request. Call us on 028 3085 0059 or email us and we will do our best to help.

Do you offer trade pricing for landscape professionals?

Yes. We work with landscape architects, contractors, local authorities, and estate managers. Contact us to discuss trade pricing and large-scale supply requirements.

Planting Services

Do you offer an on-site planting service?

Yes, for local customers we offer an on-site planting service. We come to you and plant your tree correctly, with hole size, depth, staking, and mulching all carried out to BS8545 standard.

Find out more on our Services page.

Can you assess whether a site is suitable before I plant?

Yes. We can carry out a site evaluation to assess exposure, soil type, drainage, and any other factors that could affect long-term establishment — ensuring the right tree goes in the right place from the start.
