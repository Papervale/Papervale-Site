At Papervale Trees there are a number of different services available to our customers, making sure that even a novice in gardening will be able to grow a strong and healthy tree.

Our Services

Growing support from nursery to garden

We believe that buying a tree should come with confidence: confidence that it was grown well, delivered safely, and planted properly.

From the moment you choose your tree to the day it's established in your garden or landscape, Papervale is here to help. Whether you need expert advice, reliable delivery, or hands-on planting support, we offer a range of services to suit every customer and every project.

📞

Trade customers welcome. We work with landscape architects, contractors, local authorities and estate managers. Get in touch to discuss trade pricing and large-scale supply.

Delivery

Delivered safely across the UK & Ireland

We provide a reliable delivery service to our customers, whether it be a personal delivery to a local customer or through a tracked courier to ensure our trees can be received throughout the United Kingdom and Ireland.

Along with every tree we deliver, we include a planting guide to ensure it is embedded properly and to give our customers advice on how to look after the tree, including pruning and care during its first few months.

📦
2–3 Day Tracked Courier All orders dispatched with a reliable tracked courier service, ensuring safe delivery throughout the UK and Ireland.
📋
Planting Guide Included Every delivery includes our planting and aftercare guide so your tree gets off to the best possible start.
🚚
Local Personal Delivery For local customers in Co. Down and surrounding areas, we offer a personal delivery service direct from the nursery.

Planting & Site Evaluation

Expert planting for local customers

For our local customers we also provide a planting service, during which we will go out and plant your tree for you, ensuring it is properly positioned, staked and mulched from day one.

We can also carry out a site evaluation to check how well a tree will grow in your chosen location, assessing exposure, soil type, drainage and any other factors that could affect long-term establishment.

🌱
On-Site Planting We come to you and plant your tree correctly, with hole size, depth, staking and mulching all done to BS8545 standard.
🔍
Site Evaluation We assess your site for exposure, soil type, drainage and aspect, ensuring the right tree goes in the right place.
🌿
Aftercare Advice Practical guidance on watering, feeding, mulching and ongoing care tailored to your specific tree and site.

Landscaping & Screening

Natural screening for privacy and shelter

Trees are one of the most effective and attractive ways to create privacy, reduce wind exposure, and define the boundaries of your garden or landscape. Our screening and landscaping service helps you choose and position the right species to achieve lasting, natural results.

Whether you're looking to block an unwanted view, shelter a garden from prevailing winds, or create a structured planting scheme, we can advise on the best trees for your site and help bring the vision to life.

🌲
Privacy Screening Dense, year-round screening using evergreen species chosen for your soil and climate — from classic Laurel to native Holly and Yew.
💨
Windbreak Planting Structured belts of trees and shrubs that reduce wind speed, protect crops and gardens, and improve the microclimate of your site.
🌿
Landscape Planting Schemes Species selection and placement advice for larger landscaping projects, working with your existing features, aspect and soil conditions.
